Water Damage Restoration

Fast, effective water removal and structural drying to prevent long-term damage.

Water damage is one of the most common — and most destructive — property emergencies. Whether from a burst pipe, overflowing appliance, storm flooding, or sewage backup, water can compromise structural integrity and trigger mold growth within 24–48 hours. FloodEx responds immediately to extract water, dry all affected materials, and restore your property to a safe, dry, pre-loss condition.

What's Included

24/7 emergency water extraction
Industrial dehumidification
Structural drying & monitoring
Sewage & contaminated water cleanup
Flooring & drywall removal/replacement
Moisture mapping & thermal imaging
Content drying & pack-out services
Insurance documentation & support

Fire & Smoke Damage Restoration

Full cleanup, odor removal, and structural reconstruction after fire damage.

Fire damage goes far beyond what the flames touch. Smoke, soot, and corrosive residues infiltrate every surface, cavity, and air duct in the building — and the longer they sit, the more damage they cause. FloodEx provides a complete fire restoration response: securing the structure, removing smoke and soot, eliminating odors, and rebuilding damaged areas to return your property to its pre-fire condition.

What's Included

Emergency board-up & tarping
Smoke & soot removal
Odor elimination (thermal fogging, ozone)
HVAC system cleaning
Structural damage repair
Content cleaning & restoration
Air quality testing
Full reconstruction services

Mold Remediation

Safe mold removal and prevention to protect your property and your health.

Mold can begin growing within 48 hours of a water event — and it doesn't stop. Left untreated, mold spreads through walls, ceilings, and HVAC systems, posing serious health risks and causing major structural damage. FloodEx uses certified remediation methods to safely contain, remove, and prevent mold — protecting both your building and the people inside it.

What's Included

Mold inspection & testing
Containment setup & air filtration
Safe mold removal (HEPA vacuuming)
Antimicrobial treatment
Affected material removal
Post-remediation clearance testing
Moisture source identification
Prevention recommendations
Request Mold Remediation
