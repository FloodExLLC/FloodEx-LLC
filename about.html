About FloodEx LLC

Restoring properties and rebuilding lives after disaster strikes.

Our Story

Built on a Promise to Be There When It Matters Most

FloodEx LLC was founded with a single mission: to be the restoration company people could actually count on. We saw too many property owners left stranded by companies that didn't respond fast enough — or didn't get the job done right. So we built something different.

Every member of our crew is certified, trained, and genuinely cares about the work they do.

From the moment you call, our goal is simple: get to you fast, assess the damage honestly, and restore your property to better-than-before condition — while making the whole process as stress-free as possible.
